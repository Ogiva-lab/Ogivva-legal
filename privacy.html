Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and Profile Information: Name, username, email address, phone number, password, profile details, role (e.g., gifter, receiver, vendor).
  • Identity Verification: Government ID details or verification tokens when required for compliance, fraud prevention, or vendor onboarding.
  • Contacts and Sharing: With your permission, contact details you provide for sharing moments or sending gifts.
  • Transactions and Wallet: Wallet balance, top-ups, payments, order history, and related metadata (handled via payment partners as applicable).
  • Vendor Data: Business name, address, product listings, pricing, and fulfillment metrics for vendor accounts.
  • Device and Usage Data: IP address, device identifiers, browser type, operating system, app version, pages viewed, referral URLs, and interaction events.
  • Location Data: Approximate or precise location when you permit access to location features.
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support or within the platform, and your marketing preferences.
  • Social/OAuth Data: If you sign in with third-party providers (e.g., Facebook/Meta), we receive information authorized by you via those services.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services, including orders, wallet, and vendor features.
  • Process payments and prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Personalize your experience and deliver relevant content.
  • Communicate with you about your account, transactions, security alerts, and product updates.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Conduct analytics, product research, and service improvements.
  • Send marketing communications (you may opt out at any time).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

How We Share Information

  • Vendors and Partners: Share necessary order details to fulfill purchases and deliveries.
  • Payment Processors: Share information required to process payments and handle chargebacks or disputes.
  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, customer support, email/SMS delivery, and security services under contractual safeguards.
  • Legal and Safety: Disclose when required by law, to protect rights, safety, or investigate fraud.
  • Business Transfers: As part of a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.
  • With Your Consent: We may share other information with your consent or at your direction.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Services, remember your preferences, and analyze usage. You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods vary by data type and purpose.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal information, and to data portability. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent and opt out of marketing communications.

Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

The Services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages.

International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place consistent with applicable laws.

Facebook/Meta Platform Data

If you connect your account to Facebook/Meta or use Meta-integrated features, we receive information that you authorize via Meta, such as your name and email. We use this information to authenticate you and provide Services. You can revoke access in your Facebook account settings at any time.

Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your account and personal data associated with Ogivva, you can submit a request as follows:

  1. Email support@ogivva.com with subject “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include your full name and the email or phone number associated with your account.
  3. We will verify your identity and complete the request within 30 days unless we are required to retain certain data by law.

Where supported, you may also request deletion in the app under Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated Policy with a new effective date. Your continued use of the Services after the update constitutes acceptance.
